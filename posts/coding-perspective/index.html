Deriti is currently a great chef will share how to cook fried rice  to all of you. Let’s look at what Deriti said in this story:

Before you cook fried rice please complete this tools and ingredients

Tools:

  • Stoves
  • Cooking spoon
  • Pan

Ingredients:

  • plate white rice
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Sweet soy sauce 2 tablespoon
  • Tomato sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Sambal sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Salt 1 tablespoon
  • Pepper powder ½ tablespoon
  • Chicken flavored broth 1/2 tablespoon
  • Leeks 1 stalk
  • Chicken eggs 1 grain
  • Shredded chicken 2 tablespoon
  • 1 chicken sausage
  • Margarine or cooking oil 3 tablespoon

How to cook fried rice:

  • Prepare a frying pan over medium heat, pour margarine or cooking oil.
  • Add the garlic stir-fry until fragrant.
  • Add shredded chicken and sausage, stir-fry right.
  • Pour eggs, saute together with seasoning.
  • Add the rice, stir until well blended.
  • Pour sweet soy sauce, tomato sauce, chili sauce, oysters, salt, pepper powder and powder broth.
  • Stir until the rice color changes evenly.

Do you see what’s deriti doing? Deriti try to cook fried rice with his own way. He have his concept to cook fried rice. Maybe all of you have different way to cook fried rice. That’s will same condition with coding / make. Coding is just a way to create something. The perspective of coding that i want to share to you will different with the others. So it’s up to you, if you want to follow or not. People perspective about definition of coding is different  it’s depends you see from which angle. So let’s try to see with the same angle with me so you can understand what i want to tell.

  • “Coding / How to cook” is a way how to get the expected output / product 
  • “Tools / Laptop " is just device that someone use to get the expected output / product 
  • “Knowledge / Ingredients” is something that you should have by your self to get the expected output 

So, for me if you want to start build a product you should know about the basic. What is coding, what you should prepare before you start build your product. So, if you just want to start build your product now, you should prepare and try with a “Simple fried rice” not “Expert fried rice”.

Coding is just a way, don't forget to prepare before you start build a product
