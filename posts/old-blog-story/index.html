What is startup?

Before i explain my opinion about definition of startup. I want to share my first story when build my first startup OnanOnan.com. My future goal is become a success enterpreneur and build social company.

In my mind the first opinion about startup is start a new small company, get a revenue, there is a CEO, CTO etc. That’s my first opinion about startup . So, the next question when your company is not called startup anymore?, What’s the different about startup and small business? I can’t explain about it at that time, it make me confused with that question.

I’m very difficult to acquire a new user every day, and not growth. I always ask to some mentor that i ever talk and make new strategy for acquire a new user. (To be continued …. ðŸ˜„)

Let’s move to definition about startup. For now in my opinion: Startup as temporary organization creating something new that repeatable and scalable Business Model, under conditions of uncertainly. Startup can not be separated from the business model. A business model describes how your company creates, delivers and captures value. Business Model is needed to attract investor. For a startup, People is challenge. Didn’t get any revenue but has community on your user. And don’t forget Startup all about Scale up, if you not scale up it’s small business. Startup just not think about how to get revenue but how to acquire user and make community.

5 Elements of success business:

  • Timing
  • Idea
  • Team Execution
  • Business Model
  • Funding

3 things must have by startup:

  • Core Value
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Competitors edges
